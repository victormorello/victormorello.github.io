About

I am a music producer, sound designer and computer enthusiast, specialised in synthesisers and digital audio processing.

For 5 years I have worked as a lead sound designer and synthesiser specialist for Arturia, helping define both the sound and ergonomics of several award-winning software & hardware products such as Pigments, V-Collection, MatrixBrute and PolyBrute.

Always on the lookout for unique sonic landscapes and obscure techniques, I create experimental music as Zero Crossing Point, aiming to explore complex emotions through timbre and melodies, while blurring the lines between music and sound design.

In my spare time, I like to delve into video game development and experiment with 3D graphics, photgraphy and video.
